TAKE ME TO THE GLOBAL MAKER SURVEY

Association Representatives should only complete the AREP survey.

If you are the Association Representative of your Impact Hub, this survey serves as an annual impact and performance report. This year, it also includes creating your Impact Hub profile on Good Market.

The Association Representative holds voting rights at Impact Hub Association General Assemblies, and each Impact Hub has only one designated representative.

You can pause the survey at any time—simply click Save & Continue Later in the top right corner, and you will receive a link via email to resume where you left off.
